Our Brands

Presented below are some of the brands owned by Plus Trading varying from high quality feminine products to your favorite foodstuff, all brought to you with the click of a button.

A German beverage company distributed in all Qatar for non-alcoholic drinks of any kind including Halal certified drinks. In addition, offering Private Label and Worldwide domain. Pectiv

PECTIV sanitary pads, the leading brand in the field, has the main goal of offering you a balance between your work and personal life so you don’t face any obstacles in the way of achieving your dreams and give you the best protection ever.

We have always been striving in PECTIV since our journey began in 2014 for a new generation of sanitary pads with the latest technology reached by science.

Vivacola

In 1938 Vivi Kola was brought onto the market by Mineralquelle Eglisau. Our drinks are bottled with less sugar and the best ingredients. We have set ourselves the goal of reviving the beautiful traditional Swiss brand in a modern and fresh way with great respect for the long and proud history. More than 2,500 restaurants already have Vivi Kola in their range.

Delice

Délice is a citizen brand that is very close to its consumers and cares about their health. The Group had already managed to have the confidence of market actors!

Délice now uses all its Know-How and expertise to serve consumers in order to provide them with quality products that enable them to have a healthy lifestyle. With carefully selected and collected milk from our local farmers, quality control to international standards, to serve you the best dairy products!

Rizpaille

Rizpaille is the ideal new straw for all, preserving the environment, public health and total wildlife, straws are 100% biodegradable, compostable with natural colors. Our straws totally respect the nature, they are very different from non-plastic models. Rizpaille was inspired by the leaves around spring rolls and Asian « white » noodles that are traditionally made from rice paste.
